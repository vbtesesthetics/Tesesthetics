Meet Veronica

Your Esthetician

Veronica

Hi, I'm Veronica — a licensed esthetician based in Buford, GA, originally from the beautiful city of Guadalajara, Mexico. Growing up, I was surrounded by a culture that celebrates taking care of yourself with intention and pride, and that spirit shapes everything I do.

Before esthetics, I built my career in dentistry — training as a dentist in Mexico and working as a dental assistant here in Georgia. That background gave me a deep understanding of facial anatomy, precision, and the kind of trust a client places in someone who works up close. When I'm working on your skin, I'm not guessing — I understand what's beneath it.

Making the transition into esthetics felt completely natural. The attention to detail, the care for the person in front of you, the commitment to real results — it's the same philosophy, just a different canvas. I'm just getting started here in Buford and would be honored to have you as one of my first clients. Come as you are. Leave glowing.
